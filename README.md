# TrustLens – Digital Trust Engine

### 🛡️ Digital scams exploit human psychology, not just technical vulnerabilities.

**TrustLens** is a professional-grade trust analysis engine designed to detect psychological manipulation and structural risks in digital communications. It focuses on *why* users fall for scams, providing explainable and actionable insights backed by behavioral science.

---

## 🚀 Why TrustLens Wins Hackathons

### Core Innovation
- **Behavioral Analysis**: Identifies 9+ psychological attack vectors (not just keyword matching)
- **Multi-dimensional Risk Scoring**: Content + Link + Intent analysis separately  
- **Explainable AI**: Every risk is clearly explained in human language
- **Real-time Processing**: Instant analysis with smooth animations

### Advanced Features

#### 1. **🧠 Psychological Attack Detection**
TrustLens detects sophisticated manipulation tactics:
- **Urgency Pressure**: Forces quick decisions without thinking
- **Scarcity Manipulation**: Creates fake exclusivity ("limited time")
- **Reward Bait**: Promises unrealistic gains ($$$)
- **Forced Action Triggers**: Demands immediate clicks/confirmation
- **Fear Exploitation**: Emotional manipulation via fake alerts
- **False Validation**: Fake notifications ("you've been selected")
- **Credential Harvesting**: Direct requests for passwords/PII
- **Account Hijacking**: Fake update/reactivation requests
- **Domain Spoofing**: URL manipulation techniques

#### 2. **🧬 Deep Risk Breakdown**
Granular analysis across three dimensions:
- **Content Risk** (0-100): Psychological patterns in message text
- **Link Risk** (0-100): URL structure, shorteners, obfuscation
- **Intent Risk** (0-100): Call-to-action and behavioral manipulation

#### 3. **🛡️ Smart Trust Scoring**
- **85-100**: Safe - Normal caution recommended
- **45-85**: Suspicious - Multiple warning signs detected
- **0-45**: Dangerous - High confidence threat identified

#### 4. **🔍 Explainable Results**
- Clear explanations with emojis for quick scanning
- Dynamic insights based on threat level  
- Actionable safety recommendations
- Copy-to-clipboard report functionality

#### 5. **📊 Analysis History**
- Automatic local storage of last 10 analyses
- Quick re-analysis of suspicious items
- Risk score tracking over time

#### 6. **⌨️ Power Features**
- **Character Count**: Real-time tracking (500 char max)
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Ctrl+Enter to analyze
- **Report Export**: Copy formatted analysis report
- **Demo Mode**: Pre-loaded phishing example for testing
- **Mobile Responsive**: Works on all screen sizes

## 💡 The Science Behind TrustLens

### Why Traditional Detection Fails
Traditional systems look for:
- ❌ Known malicious signatures (new variants bypass these)
- ❌ Blacklisted URLs (attackers use fresh domains)  
- ❌ Spam keywords (context matters!)

### What TrustLens Does Differently
TrustLens analyzes the **Intent** behind the message:

1. **Content Patterns**: Emotional triggers + pressure tactics
2. **Structural Risks**: Malformed URLs, shorteners, spoofing indicators
3. **Psychological Manipulation**: Behavioral exploitation detection
4. **Weighted Scoring**: Multiple factors contribute appropriately

### Key Insight
> "Scams succeed because they manufacture trust. TrustLens deconstructs that process in real-time."
